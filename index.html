The Trade List

What gets done

Painting

Interior and exterior, cut lines you can trust, finishes that hold up past the first season.

Drywall

Hanging, taping, mudding, and patch repair — finished smooth and ready for paint.

Flooring

Hardwood, laminate, and vinyl plank installs, plus repairs to floors already down.

Decks

Built, repaired, and resurfaced — decks that hold up to weather and to years of use.

Tile

Backsplashes, showers, and floors — laid level, spaced even, and grouted clean.

Renovations

Kitchen and bath updates, room conversions, and full remodel projects start to finish.

Home Maintenance

The running list every homeowner keeps putting off — finally getting crossed out.

Lock Installation

New locks, re-keys, and hardware swaps for doors that need to actually lock right.
